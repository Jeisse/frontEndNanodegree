Terra Networks

TerraTV Project

Terra TV is the product for on demand video of Terra portal. The goal was to create a video experience that is more integrated with other contents without loosing TerraTV's brand recognition by users and advertisers. The redesign highlights featured videos and gives a full navigation to all video channels. A color code identifies the main channels and guides users to the subjects of interest.

The product experience was also thought outside of the portal. New functionalities were added to the player to allow embed and sharing, the video now can be watched in the user's Facebook feed, increasing content distribution. The project includes desktop and mobile.

FlexMaint

FlexMaint Project

FlexMaint is a browser based Preventive Maintenance CMMS Software. With it’s ultimate flexibility, it can be customised by the user. Apart from a few selected mandatory items, every other field on the screen can be turned on or off, made mandatory or not, or read only or not. FlexMaint™ also allows clients total profile control including the asset tree structure, thereby limiting the user privileges by only allowing access to certain assets.
